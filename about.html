About Us

Two generations, one family, one crew you can call by name.

Mr. Koo's Heating & Air is a family-owned HVAC company built on a simple idea: treat every Atlanta home like it's your own.

Our story

Started with one truck and a promise to show up.

Mr. Koo's Heating & Air started as a one-truck operation with a simple standard: answer the phone, show up when you say you will, and never leave a job worse than you found it. Two generations later, that's still the whole business model.

We're not a franchise and we're not backed by a private equity rollup. Every technician on our team is trained, licensed, and treated like family — because most of them are.
