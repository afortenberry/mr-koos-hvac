Signs you need us

If your system is doing any of this, call.

Blowing warm air

Vents running but the air isn't cold, or cools then stops.

Weak or no airflow

Some rooms barely get air, or the whole system feels weak.

Grinding, squealing, or clicking

New or worsening noise from the outdoor or indoor unit.

Short cycling

System turns on and off in quick bursts instead of full cycles.

Water pooling near the unit

A clogged condensate line can cause leaks and water damage.

Rising energy bills

A struggling system often uses more power for less cooling.
