Signs you need us

What tells us it's time to call.

No heat, or cold air from vents

Furnace runs but the air isn't warm, or it never kicks on.

Short cycling

Heat turns on and off in short bursts instead of steady cycles.

Unusual smells or sounds

A brief dusty smell at season's first run is normal; anything else, get it checked.

Uneven heat

Some rooms warm, others stay cold no matter the thermostat setting.

Pilot light or ignition issues

Pilot won't stay lit, or the system fails to ignite consistently.

Rising heating bills

A struggling furnace often burns more fuel for less warmth.
